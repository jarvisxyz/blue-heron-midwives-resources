# Blue Heron Midwives Connecting Pregnancy Program - Resource Index

## Overview
This repository contains summaries of all resources from the Blue Heron Midwives Connecting Pregnancy Program website. Resources are organized by session/topic area for easy navigation.

## Session Organization

### [Session 1-3: Pregnancy Fundamentals](session_1_3/)
**40 resources covering:**
- Nutrition and healthy eating during pregnancy
- Exercise and physical activity
- Emotional health and mental wellness
- Labour preparation and understanding

**Resources:** 1-40

**Resources covering:**

- When to seek medical intervention

**Resources:** 1-8

## Canadian Health Resources Referenced

- **Public Health Agency of Canada (PHAC)** - Government health guidelines
- **Health Canada** - Official health recommendations
- **pregnancyinfo.ca** - Canadian pregnancy information
- **ontariomidwives.ca** - College of Midwives of Ontario
- **regionofwaterloo.ca** - Local health services
- **Grand River Hospital** - Hospital admission procedures

## Notes

- All resources are tailored for Canadian audiences
- Content aligns with College of Midwives of Ontario guidelines
- Resources emphasize evidence-based, holistic approaches
- Warnings and contraindications are clearly marked
- Resources focus on Waterloo Region community support
