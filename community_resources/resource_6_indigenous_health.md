## Summary

This resource provides information about Indigenous health services and cultural support available in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Indigenous Health Services:**
  - Indigenous health workers
  - Cultural support services
  - Traditional healing practices
  - Pregnancy and parenting support
  - Connection to community

- **Services Available:**
  - Health assessments
  - Referrals to specialized care
  - Cultural ceremonies
  - Elders consultation
  - Family support

- **How to Access:**
  - Contact Indigenous health organizations
  - Referral from healthcare provider
  - Self-referral to some programs
  - Community events and drop-ins

- **Cultural Safety:**
  - Culturally appropriate care
  - Respect for traditions
  - Language support
  - Community connection
  - Holistic health approach

- **Organizations:**
  - Indigenous Health Centre
  - Aboriginal Health Organizations
  - Community-based programs
  - Traditional healing circles

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports cultural identity
- Provides culturally safe care
- Connects to community
- Enhances pregnancy outcomes
- Reduces barriers to care
- Promotes holistic health

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Cultural safety is essential:** You deserve respectful care
- **Traditional practices are valid:** Integrate with medical care
- **Support available:** Indigenous health workers
- **Community resources:** Connect with Indigenous organizations
- **Advocacy:** Always available if needed
- **Healing:** Address historical trauma with support
