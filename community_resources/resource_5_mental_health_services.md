# Resource 5: Mental Health Services

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Mental Health Services  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 5 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers mental health services available in Waterloo Region for pregnant and postpartum individuals and families.

### Key Points

- **Perinatal Mental Health Services:**
  - Perinatal mental health teams
  - Counseling and therapy
  - Support groups
  - Medication management
  - Crisis intervention

- **General Mental Health Services:**
  - Community mental health teams
  - Individual counseling
  - Group therapy
  - Crisis services
  - Psychiatric services

- **How to Access:**
  - Referral from family doctor
  - Self-referral to some services
  - Crisis line for immediate help
  - Walk-in clinics available

- **Cost:**
  - Many services free through OHIP
  - Some may have fees
  - Sliding scale available
  - Insurance may cover some services

- **Specialized Programs:**
  - Postpartum depression treatment
  - Anxiety and depression programs
  - Trauma-informed care
  - Parenting support

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports mental health during pregnancy
- Reduces risk of postpartum depression
- Enhances parent-infant bonding
- Improves family functioning
- Provides crisis support
- Promotes recovery

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Crisis line:** 988 Suicide Crisis Helpline available 24/7
- **Wait times:** May vary depending on service
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Medication:** Discuss with provider if breastfeeding
- **Resources:** Mental health navigators available
