# Resource 14: Housing Support

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Housing Support  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 14 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers housing support services available for pregnant and parenting families in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Housing Assistance:**
  - Emergency shelter referrals
  - Transitional housing
  - Affordable housing applications
  - Housing stability support
  - Homelessness prevention

- **Support Services:**
  - Housing navigation
  - Rental assistance
  - Utility assistance
  - Furniture and household items
  - Move-in support

- **How to Access:**
  - Contact social services
  - Referral from healthcare provider
  - Self-referral to housing programs
  - Emergency shelter contacts

- **Eligibility:**
  - Based on need and circumstances
  - Some programs for all families
  - Some for families experiencing homelessness
  - Income-based eligibility for some

- **Resources:**
  - Housing Ontario
  - Local housing authorities
  - Community housing providers

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Provides stable housing for pregnancy
- Supports healthy family environment
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Improves access to healthcare
- Enhances child development
- Promotes family stability

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Emergency housing:** Available 24/7 if needed
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Wait lists:** Some housing may have wait times
- **Application:** Complete all required forms
- **Resources:** Housing navigators available

### Canadian Resources Referenced

- Housing Ontario
- Region of Waterloo Housing Services
- Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation (CMHC)
