# Resource 10: Home Visiting Programs

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Home Visiting Programs  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 10 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers home visiting programs that provide in-home support to new families in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Types of Home Visiting:**
  - Public health nursing visits
  - Social service home visits
  - Parent support home visits
  - Specialized family support
  - Infant development visits

- **Services Provided:**
  - Infant health checks
  - Parenting support
  - Resource connection
  - Home safety assessment
  - Development screening

- **How to Access:**
  - Referral from healthcare provider
  - Self-referral to some programs
  - Registration required for some
  - Drop-in home visiting available

- **Eligibility:**
  - Varies by program
  - Some for all new parents
  - Some for families in need
  - Some for high-risk families

- **Cost:**
  - Many programs free
  - Some may have fees
  - Financial assistance available

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Provides in-home support
- Reduces isolation
- Enhances parenting skills
- Connects to community resources
- Promotes child development
- Improves family well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Wait times:** May vary depending on program
- **Eligibility:** Some programs have specific criteria
- **Accessibility:** Home visits accommodate schedules
- **Resources:** Program coordinators can help navigate services

### Canadian Resources Referenced
