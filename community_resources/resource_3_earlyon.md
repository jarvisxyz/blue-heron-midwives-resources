# Resource 3: EarlyON Child and Family Centres

## Summary

This resource covers EarlyON Child and Family Centres, which provide programs and services for families with young children in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Programs Offered:**
  - Drop-in programs
  - Parent-child groups
  - Parent education workshops
  - Playgroups
  - Specialized support programs

- **Services for Different Ages:**
  - Prenatal to age 2: Parent and child programs
  - Ages 3-8: School readiness programs
  - All ages: Family support services

- **Locations in Waterloo Region:**
  - Kitchener
  - Waterloo
  - Cambridge
  - Woolwich
  - Additional locations

- **How to Access:**
  - Drop-in without registration
  - Register for programs
  - Contact centre directly
  - Online registration available

- **Cost:**
  - Most programs free
  - Some may have nominal fees
  - Financial assistance available

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports early child development
- Connects parents to community
- Provides parenting support
- Reduces isolation
- Enhances family functioning
- Promotes healthy development

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **COVID protocols:** Check current requirements
- **Wait lists:** Some programs may have wait times
- **Eligibility:** Some programs have age restrictions
- **Accessibility:** Centres are accessible
- **Resources:** Staff can help find appropriate programs
