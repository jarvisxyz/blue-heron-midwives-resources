## Summary

This resource provides information about health services available through the Region of Waterloo Public Health Department.

### Key Points

- **Pregnancy and Parenting Services:**
  - Prenatal education classes
  - New parent groups
  - Breastfeeding support
  - Child development screening
  - Immunization services

- **Home Visiting Programs:**
  - New parent home visits
  - Infant health checks
  - Parenting support
  - Resource connection

- **Public Health Nursing:**
  - Immunization clinics
  - Health assessments
  - Disease prevention
  - Health education

- **Community Programs:**
  - EarlyON programs
  - Parent education workshops
  - Health promotion activities
  - Community events

- **How to Access Services:**
  - Contact public health office
  - Register for programs
  - Attend drop-in sessions
  - Request home visits

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Provides free or low-cost services
- Supports healthy pregnancy outcomes
- Enhances parenting skills
- Connects to community resources
- Promotes child development
- Reduces isolation

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Services may have wait times:** Register early
- **Eligibility:** Some services have specific criteria
- **Confidentiality:** Health information is protected
- **Cost:** Many services are free
- **Contact information:** Available on Region of Waterloo website
- **Resources:** Public health nurses can help navigate services
