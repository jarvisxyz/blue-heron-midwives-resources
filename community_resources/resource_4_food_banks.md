# Resource 4: Waterloo Region Food Banks

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Waterloo Region Food Banks  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 4 of 15)

## Summary

This resource provides information about food banks and nutrition support programs available in Waterloo Region for families in need.

### Key Points

- **Food Bank Services:**
  - Emergency food hampers
  - Regular food assistance
  - Special programs for pregnant and parenting families
  - Fresh produce programs
  - Baby food and formula assistance

- **How to Access:**
  - Referral from social service agency
  - Self-referral to some locations
  - Drop-in hours vary
  - Appointment may be required

- **Additional Support:**
  - Nutrition education
  - Cooking classes
  - Budget management workshops
  - Connection to other resources

- **Eligibility:**
  - Based on income and need
  - No strict requirements at some locations
  - Confidential service
  - All families welcome

- **Locations:**
  - Multiple locations throughout Waterloo Region
  - Some offer delivery services
  - Some have mobile food banks

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports healthy nutrition during pregnancy
- Ensures baby receives adequate food
- Reduces food insecurity
- Provides nutrition education
- Connects to other support services
- Improves family well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **No judgment:** Services available to all who need them
- **Bring ID:** Some locations require identification
- **Hours vary:** Check before visiting
- **Resources:** Social workers can help navigate services

### Canadian Resources Referenced
