# Resource 9: Postpartum Depression Support

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Postpartum Depression Support  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 9 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers postpartum depression support services and resources available in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Support Services:**
  - Postpartum depression counseling
  - Support groups
  - Peer support programs
  - Crisis intervention
  - Family therapy

- **How to Access:**
  - Referral from healthcare provider
  - Self-referral to some services
  - Crisis line for immediate help
  - Walk-in clinics available

- **Programs Available:**
  - Perinatal mental health teams
  - Parent-infant interaction programs
  - Mother-baby programs
  - Father/partner support
  - Online resources

- **Cost:**
  - Many services free through OHIP
  - Some may have fees
  - Sliding scale available
  - Insurance may cover some services

- **Crisis Resources:**
  - 988 Suicide Crisis Helpline
  - Postpartum Support International Canada
  - Emergency services if needed

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports mental health recovery
- Enhances parent-infant bonding
- Improves family functioning
- Reduces risk of long-term mental health issues
- Provides crisis support
- Promotes recovery

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Emergency help:** If thoughts of self-harm, call 911 or crisis line immediately
- **Don't wait:** Early treatment prevents worsening
- **Partner mental health:** Also affected (5-10%)
- **Medication decisions:** Discuss with provider
- **Recovery is possible:** Most parents recover with treatment
- **Resources available:** Help is accessible

### Canadian Resources Referenced

- Postpartum Support International Canada
- Waterloo Region Mental Health and Addictions
- Canadian Perinatal Mental Health Alliance
