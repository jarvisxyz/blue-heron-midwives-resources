# Resource 13: Transportation Services

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Transportation Services  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 13 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers transportation services available for pregnant and parenting families in Waterloo Region who need assistance getting to appointments and activities.

### Key Points

- **Public Transit:**
  - Grand River Transit (GRT)
  - Reduced fare for seniors and youth
  - Free transit for some programs
  - Accessible vehicles available

- **Specialized Transportation:**
  - Accessibility Services for people with disabilities
  - Non-emergency medical transportation
  - Family transportation assistance
  - Volunteer driver programs

- **How to Access:**
  - Register for specialized services
  - Book appointments in advance
  - Contact transit authority
  - Self-referral to some programs

- **Eligibility:**
  - Varies by program
  - Some for all community members
  - Some for families with specific needs
  - Income-based eligibility for some

- **Cost:**
  - Public transit: Standard fare
  - Specialized services: May be free or reduced cost
  - Financial assistance available

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Enables access to healthcare appointments
- Supports attendance at support groups
- Reduces isolation
- Improves access to resources
- Enhances family functioning
- Promotes health and well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Book in advance:** Specialized transportation requires scheduling
- **Accessibility:** Check vehicle accessibility needs
- **Cost:** Some services may have fees
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Resources:** Transportation coordinators available
