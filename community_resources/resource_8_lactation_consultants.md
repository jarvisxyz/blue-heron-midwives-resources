# Resource 8: Lactation Consultants

**Resource Title:** Lactation Consultants  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 8 of 15)

## Summary

This resource provides information about lactation consultants and breastfeeding support services available in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Lactation Consultant Services:**
  - Latching assistance
  - Feeding problem solving
  - Milk supply concerns
  - Pumping guidance
  - Weaning support

- **How to Access:**
  - Referral from midwife or doctor
  - Self-referral to private consultants
  - Public health breastfeeding support
  - Hospital-based services

- **Credentials:**
  - International Board Certified Lactation Consultants (IBCLC)
  - Registered Lactation Consultants (RLC)
  - Certified Lactation Counselors (CLC)

- **Cost:**
  - Some covered by insurance
  - Some covered by OHIP through public health
  - Private consultants may charge fees
  - Sliding scale available

- **Support Options:**
  - One-on-one appointments
  - Group classes
  - Drop-in breastfeeding clinics
  - Home visits
  - Virtual consultations

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports successful breastfeeding
- Resolves feeding problems
- Enhances parent-infant bonding
- Reduces early cessation
- Improves breastfeeding duration
- Provides evidence-based support

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Seek help early:** Don't wait until problems become severe
- **Credentials matter:** Look for certified consultants
- **Insurance:** Check coverage before appointments
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Resources:** Breastfeeding helplines available
