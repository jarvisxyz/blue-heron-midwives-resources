## Summary

This resource provides information about Parent Line, a free parenting support service available in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Services Offered:**
  - Parenting information and resources
  - One-on-one support calls
  - Group programs
  - EarlyON connections
  - Referrals to community resources

- **How to Access:**
  - Phone: 1-800-464-2258 (Ontario-wide)
  - Local Waterloo Region number
  - Email support
  - In-person appointments

- **Topics Covered:**
  - Child development
  - Behavior management
  - Feeding and nutrition
  - Sleep issues
  - Parent self-care

- **Target Audience:**
  - Parents of children 0-12 years
  - Expectant parents
  - Caregivers
  - Families in need of support

- **Cost:**
  - Free service
  - No registration required
  - Confidential

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Supports new parents
- Reduces isolation
- Provides evidence-based information
- Connects to community resources
- Enhances parenting confidence
- Improves family well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Not crisis line:** For emergencies, call 911
- **Wait times:** May vary depending on demand
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Multilingual:** Some services available in other languages
- **Resources:** Additional support available if needed
