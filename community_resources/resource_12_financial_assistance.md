# Resource 12: Financial Assistance Programs

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/community-resources--newborn-info.html  
**Resource Title:** Financial Assistance Programs  
**Session:** Community Resources (Resource 12 of 15)

## Summary

This resource covers financial assistance programs available for pregnant and parenting families in Waterloo Region.

### Key Points

- **Government Benefits:**
  - Canada Child Benefit (CCB)
  - Ontario Child Benefit (OCB)
  - Goods and Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax (GST/HST) credit
  - Provincial benefits

- **Local Financial Assistance:**
  - Emergency financial assistance
  - Baby supplies assistance
  - Clothing vouchers
  - Formula and food assistance
  - Transportation assistance

- **How to Access:**
  - Apply through Service Canada
  - Contact local social services
  - Self-referral to some programs
  - Referral from healthcare provider

- **Eligibility:**
  - Based on income and family size
  - Some programs have no income requirements
  - Citizenship/residency requirements
  - Documentation required

- **Application Process:**
  - Complete application forms
  - Provide required documentation
  - Interview may be required
  - Decision typically within specified timeframe

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Reduces financial stress
- Ensures baby receives adequate resources
- Supports family stability
- Improves access to necessities
- Reduces poverty risk
- Enhances family well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Apply early:** Some benefits take time to process
- **Documentation:** Have required documents ready
- **Reporting changes:** Inform authorities of income changes
- **Confidential:** Your information is protected
- **Resources:** Social workers can help with applications

### Canadian Resources Referenced

- Service Canada: Child Benefits
- Ontario Ministry of Children, Community and Social Services
