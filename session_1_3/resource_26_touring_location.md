# Resource 26: Touring Your Birth Location

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/session-1-3.html  
**Resource Title:** Touring Your Birth Location  
**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 26 of 40)

## Summary

This resource provides guidance on touring and preparing for your birth location, whether hospital, birth centre, or home, to reduce anxiety and increase comfort.

### Key Points

- **Why Tour:**
  - Reduces anxiety about unfamiliar environment
  - Helps visualize birth experience
  - Identifies practical considerations
  - Builds confidence in birth location
  - Allows time to ask questions

- **What to Look For:**
  - **Labor rooms:** Size, bed type, comfort features
  - **Bathroom:** Shower, tub access
  - **Support person space:** Where can partner stay?
  - **Parking:** How to access building
  - **Visiting hours:** Who can visit after birth
  - **Feeding areas:** Private spaces for breastfeeding
  - **Equipment:** What's available (birth balls, stools)

- **Questions to Ask:**
  - What are typical wait times?
  - Can I move around during labour?
  - What food and drink options available?
  - How many people can support me?
  - What happens if I need to transfer?
  - How is the baby examined after birth?

- **Hospital Tours:**
  - Schedule in advance
  - Ask about current availability
  - Note differences between rooms
  - Check out postpartum rooms
  - Locate key areas (nursery, cafeteria)

- **Birth Centre Tours:**
  - Understand eligibility requirements
  - See birth rooms and equipment
  - Learn about transfer procedures
  - Meet staff if possible

- **Home Birth Preparation:**
  - Plan birth space setup
  - Identify backup hospital
  - Practice with birth ball and equipment
  - Plan for overnight stay

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Reduces birth-related anxiety
- Increases comfort and confidence
- Helps make informed decisions
- Prepares support people
- Reduces surprises during labour
- Improves birth experience

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Schedule early:** Tours fill up quickly
- **Bring partner:** They'll need to know location
- **Take notes or photos:** If allowed
- **Visit at different times:** Day vs. evening may differ
- **Follow COVID protocols:** Check current restrictions
- **Don't stress:** Tour is informational, not commitment
