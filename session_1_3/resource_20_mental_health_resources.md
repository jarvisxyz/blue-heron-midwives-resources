## Summary

This resource provides a comprehensive directory of mental health resources available to pregnant and postpartum individuals in Canada, including crisis lines, counseling services, and support programs.

### Key Points

- **Crisis Resources:**
  - Canada Suicide Prevention Service: 1-833-456-4566
  - Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 686868
  - Kids Help Phone: 1-800-668-6868 (for younger parents)
  - 988 Suicide Crisis Helpline (new national number)

- **Perinatal Mental Health Services:**
  - Perinatal Mental Health Helpline: 1-800-661-8667
  - Postpartum Support International Canada
  - Provincial perinatal mental health programs
  - Hospital-based perinatal programs

- **Counseling and Therapy:**
  - College of Psychologists of Ontario referrals
  - Registered Social Workers
  - Family therapists
  - Online therapy platforms
  - Sliding scale options

- **Support Groups:**
  - Parenting groups
  - Postpartum depression support
  - Pregnancy loss groups
  - New parent groups
  - Online communities

- **Online Resources:**
  - Mental Health Commission of Canada
  - Health Canada mental health pages
  - Pregnancyinfo.ca mental health section
  - Apps for mental wellness

- **Healthcare Provider Resources:**
  - Midwife counseling services
  - Family doctor referrals
  - Psychiatrist consultations
  - Community health centers

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Early intervention prevents worsening symptoms
- Supports healthy pregnancy outcomes
- Reduces risk of postpartum depression
- Improves parent-infant bonding
- Enhances family functioning
- Provides crisis support when needed

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Emergency situations:** Call 911 or go to emergency department
- **Crisis lines are free and confidential**
- **Medication decisions:** Discuss with healthcare provider
- **Many resources are free or covered by insurance**
- **Don't wait for crisis:** Seek help early
- **Resources vary by province:** Check local options
