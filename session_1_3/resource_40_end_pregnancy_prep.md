# Resource 40: End of Pregnancy Preparation

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/session-1-3.html  
**Resource Title:** End of Pregnancy Preparation  
**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 40 of 40)

## Summary

This resource helps prepare for the final weeks of pregnancy, recognizing signs that birth is near, and finalizing birth plans.

### Key Points

- **Final Weeks Preparation:**
  - Confirm birth plan details
  - Ensure hospital bag is packed
  - Install car seat
  - Plan transportation to birth location
  - Arrange postpartum support

- **Signs Birth is Near:**
  - Baby dropping (lightening)
  - Increased energy (nesting)
  - More frequent contractions (practice)
  - Cervical changes
  - Bloody show

- **Final Appointments:**
  - Weekly checkups after 36 weeks
  - Group B strep screening (36-37 weeks)
  - Baby's position check
  - Birth plan discussion
  - Postpartum planning

- **Mental Preparation:**
  - Visualize birth positively
  - Practice relaxation techniques
  - Discuss birth preferences with partner
  - Address remaining concerns
  - Build confidence

- **Postpartum Planning:**
  - Arrange help at home
  - Plan meals for early weeks
  - Prepare nursery
  - Organize support network
  - Schedule postpartum appointments

- **When to Go to Birth Location:**
  - Follow midwife's instructions
  - Usually when contractions are regular and strong
  - If water breaks
  - If concerned about baby's movement
  - If you feel you can't cope at home

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Reduces anxiety about birth
- Ensures practical preparations complete
- Enhances confidence for labour
- Supports informed decision-making
- Improves birth experience
- Prepares for postpartum period

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Call midwife:** When labour signs appear
- **Don't wait:** Go to birth location when instructed
- **Trust your body:** Labour will begin when ready
- **Stay calm:** Final weeks can feel long
- **Flexibility:** Plans may change
- **Support available:** Don't hesitate to ask for help
