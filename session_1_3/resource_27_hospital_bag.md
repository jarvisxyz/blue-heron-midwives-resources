# Resource 27: Packing Your Hospital Bag

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/session-1-3.html  
**Resource Title:** Packing Your Hospital Bag  
**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 27 of 40)

## Summary

This resource provides a comprehensive checklist for packing your hospital or birth center bag, ensuring you have everything needed for labour, birth, and postpartum recovery.

### Key Points

- **For Parent:**
  - **Clothing:** Robe, comfortable clothes for discharge, nursing bras, slippers
  - **Toiletries:** Toothbrush, toothpaste, hairbrush, lip balm, face wipes
  - **Documents:** ID, health card, birth plan, medical records
  - **Comfort items:** Birth ball, pillows, blankets, music player
  - **Food and drink:** Snacks, electrolyte drinks, straw
  - **Entertainment:** Books, tablet, phone charger

- **For Baby:**
  - **Clothing:** 2-3 outfits, hats, socks, swaddles
  - **Diapers:** Newborn size (few)
  - **Car seat:** Required for discharge
  - **Blankets:** Receiving blankets, swaddles
  - **Feeding:** Breast pump if needed, formula if supplementing

- **For Partner/Support Person:**
  - **Clothing:** Comfortable clothes, change of clothes
  - **Toiletries:** Basic items
  - **Food:** Snacks, meals
  - **Entertainment:** Books, games
  - **Camera:** To capture birth

- **Important Documents:**
  - Health card and ID
  - Birth plan (copies)
  - Medical records
  - Insurance information
  - Contact list

- **Timing:**
  - Pack by 36 weeks
  - Keep in accessible location
  - Have backup bag for partner
  - Update as needed

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Reduces stress during labour
- Ensures comfort during birth
- Helps focus on birth rather than packing
- Prevents forgetting important items
- Supports partner's comfort
- Makes discharge smoother

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Don't wait until labour starts:** Pack early
- **Keep car seat ready:** Install and check before birth
- **Follow hospital guidelines:** Some items may not be allowed
- **Have digital copies:** Of important documents
- **Update list:** As pregnancy progresses
- **Practice:** With car seat installation
