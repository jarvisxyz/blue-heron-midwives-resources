# Resource 38: Preparing for Twins/Multiples

**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 38 of 40)

## Summary

This resource provides practical guidance for preparing for and caring for twins or multiples, including equipment, routines, and support needs.

### Key Points

- **Equipment Needs:**
  - Double stroller or two single strollers
  - Two car seats (or compatible system)
  - Extra feeding supplies
  - Additional bedding and clothing
  - Twin-specific products (double diaper pail, etc.)

- **Feeding Considerations:**
  - Breastfeeding multiples is possible
  - May need extra support
  - Bottle feeding options
  - Feeding schedules
  - Nutrition for milk production

- **Sleep Strategies:**
  - Synchronized sleep schedules
  - Safe sleep for multiples
  - Room sharing considerations
  - Getting rest as parent
  - Help from support people

- **Daily Routines:**
  - Feeding schedules
  - Diaper changes
  - Bathing routines
  - Tummy time
  - Developmental activities

- **Support Needs:**
  - Extra help at home
  - Meal preparation
  - Household management
  - Emotional support
  - Parent groups for multiples

- **Financial Considerations:**
  - Increased costs for supplies
  - Insurance coverage
  - Government benefits
  - Budget planning

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Prepares parents for practical challenges
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Enhances ability to care for babies
- Supports healthy development
- Improves family functioning
- Promotes parental well-being

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Plan ahead:** Order equipment early
- **Ask for help:** Don't try to do everything alone
- **Connect with other multiples parents:** Learn from experience
- **Take care of yourself:** Parenting multiples is demanding
- **Resources available:** Support groups, programs
- **Flexibility:** Routines will evolve
