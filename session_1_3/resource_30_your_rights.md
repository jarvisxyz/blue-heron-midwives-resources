# Resource 30: Understanding Your Rights

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/session-1-3.html  
**Resource Title:** Understanding Your Rights  
**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 30 of 40)

## Summary

This resource outlines the rights of pregnant and birthing people in Canada, including informed consent, cultural safety, and access to care.

### Key Points

- **Informed Consent:**
  - Right to complete information about procedures
  - Right to understand risks and benefits
  - Right to ask questions
  - Right to refuse treatment
  - Right to change mind at any time

- **Cultural Safety and Respect:**
  - Right to culturally appropriate care
  - Right to interpreter services
  - Right to respect for beliefs and values
  - Right to non-discriminatory care
  - Right to privacy and dignity

- **Access to Care:**
  - Right to midwifery care (Ontario)
  - Right to appropriate medical care
  - Right to second opinions
  - Right to continuity of care
  - Right to emergency services

- **Privacy and Confidentiality:**
  - Right to privacy during examinations
  - Right to confidentiality of medical information
  - Right to control who is present
  - Right to access your medical records

- **Documentation and Records:**
  - Right to access your medical records
  - Right to correct inaccurate information
  - Right to understand documentation
  - Right to copies of records

- **Advocacy Resources:**
  - Patient advocates
  - Ombudsperson services
  - Professional regulatory colleges
  - Legal aid if needed

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Empowers informed decision-making
- Reduces fear and anxiety
- Improves quality of care
- Prevents unwanted interventions
- Supports positive birth experience
- Enhances trust in healthcare system

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Know your rights:** They protect you
- **Don't hesitate to ask:** Questions are your right
- **Documentation:** Keep records of conversations
- **Advocacy:** Always available if needed
- **Cultural safety:** Request if needed
- **Legal resources:** Available if rights violated
