# Resource 28: Partner Preparation

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/session-1-3.html  
**Resource Title:** Partner Preparation  
**Session:** Session 1-3 (Resource 28 of 40)

## Summary

This resource helps partners prepare for labour and birth, understanding their role, how to support, and managing their own emotions.

### Key Points

- **Understanding Your Role:**
  - Primary support person
  - Advocate for birthing parent
  - Communicate with healthcare team
  - Provide physical comfort
  - Make decisions if needed

- **Preparing for Labour:**
  - Attend all prenatal appointments
  - Learn about labour stages
  - Practice comfort measures
  - Understand pain management options
  - Know when to call midwife

- **Support Techniques:**
  - **Physical:** Massage, counter-pressure, positioning
  - **Emotional:** Reassurance, encouragement, presence
  - **Practical:** Timing contractions, hydration, food
  - **Advocacy:** Ask questions, clarify options

- **Communication:**
  - Discuss birth preferences together
  - Understand birth plan
  - Know when to speak up
  - Respect birthing parent's wishes
  - Stay calm and supportive

- **Managing Your Emotions:**
  - It's normal to feel anxious
  - Practice self-care
  - Discuss concerns with partner
  - Seek support if needed
  - Remember: Your calm helps your partner

- **After Birth:**
  - Support bonding and breastfeeding
  - Help with newborn care
  - Take care of yourself
  - Share responsibilities
  - Watch for postpartum depression

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Improves birth experience
- Reduces birthing parent's anxiety
- Enhances partner confidence
- Supports healthy family dynamics
- Improves postpartum adjustment
- Strengthens relationship

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Don't be passive:** Active participation helps
- **Take care of yourself:** You can't support if exhausted
- **Stay informed:** Ask questions
- **Be flexible:** Birth may not go as planned
- **Postpartum mental health:** Watch for signs in yourself too
- **Resources available:** Don't struggle alone
