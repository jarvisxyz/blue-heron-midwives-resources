## Summary

This resource covers essential newborn care skills and knowledge for new parents.

### Key Points

- **Feeding:**
  - Newborns feed 8-12 times daily
  - Watch for hunger cues
  - Burp frequently
  - Track wet and dirty diapers

- **Diapering:**
  - Newborns need 10+ diaper changes daily
  - Change frequently to prevent rash
  - Wipe front to back (girls)
  - Apply barrier cream if needed

- **Cord Care:**
  - Keep cord clean and dry
  - Fold diaper below cord
  - Air dry when possible
  - Watch for signs of infection

- **Temperature Regulation:**
  - Dress in layers
  - Check temperature by touching chest
  - Avoid overheating
  - Safe sleep environment

- **Normal Newborn Behaviors:**
  - Sleeps 16-18 hours daily
  - Frequent feeding
  - Startles easily (Moro reflex)
  - Irregular breathing patterns

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Essential skills for new parents
- Supports healthy newborn development
- Reduces anxiety about baby care
- Enhances confidence
- Promotes bonding
- Ensures baby's safety and comfort

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Call doctor if:** Fever over 38°C, poor feeding, lethargy
- **Safe sleep:** Always on back, firm surface
- **Don't shake baby:** Shaken baby syndrome is dangerous
- **Take care of yourself:** Rest when baby rests
- **Ask for help:** Don't struggle alone
- **Resources:** New parent classes available
