# Resource 1: Hospital Admission Form

**Resource Title:** Hospital Admission Form  
**Session:** GRH Forms (Resource 1 of 5)

## Summary

This resource covers the hospital admission form required for Grand River Hospital, including what information is needed and how to complete it.

### Key Points

- **Required Information:**
  - Personal information (name, date of birth, health card number)
  - Emergency contact information
  - Medical history
  - Current medications
  - Allergies
  - Previous surgical history

- **Pregnancy-Specific Information:**
  - Due date
  - Prenatal care provider
  - Any pregnancy complications
  - Current medications and supplements
  - Group B strep status
  - Blood type and Rh status

- **Birth Preferences:**
  - Pain management preferences
  - Who should be present during birth
  - Cultural or religious considerations
  - Feeding preferences

- **How to Complete:**
  - Fill out legibly
  - Provide accurate information
  - Update if information changes
  - Return by specified deadline
  - Keep copies for your records

- **When to Submit:**
  - By 36 weeks of pregnancy
  - Before hospital admission if possible
  - Bring updated copy to hospital

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Required for hospital admission
- Ensures accurate medical information
- Supports continuity of care
- Reduces delays during admission
- Enhances safety
- Improves hospital experience

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Complete accurately:** Incomplete forms may delay admission
- **Update information:** If changes occur
- **Keep copies:** For your records
- **Bring to hospital:** Even if submitted online
- **Questions:** Contact hospital if unclear
- **Resources:** Midwife can help complete forms
