# Resource 5: Postpartum Care Agreement

**Source URL:** https://bhmconnectingpregnancy.weebly.com/grh-pre-admit-forms.html  
**Resource Title:** Postpartum Care Agreement  
**Session:** GRH Forms (Resource 5 of 5)

## Summary

This resource covers the postpartum care agreement and discharge planning forms required by Grand River Hospital.

### Key Points

- **Postpartum Care Plan:**
  - Expected length of stay
  - Newborn care instructions
  - Feeding plan (breast or formula)
  - Pain management after birth
  - Warning signs to watch for

- **Discharge Planning:**
  - When discharge is expected
  - Transportation arrangements
  - Home support needs
  - Follow-up appointments
  - Required equipment (car seat)

- **Newborn Care Instructions:**
  - Feeding schedule
  - Diapering and hygiene
  - Sleep safety
  - When to call doctor
  - Vitamin K and hepatitis B shots

- **Maternal Care Instructions:**
  - Perineal or C-section care
  - Bleeding expectations
  - Activity restrictions
  - Medication instructions
  - Warning signs

- **Follow-Up Care:**
  - Postpartum checkup timing
  - Baby's pediatrician appointment
  - Mental health screening
  - Contraception counseling
  - Support resources

### Relevance to Pregnancy/Birth

- Ensures smooth hospital discharge
- Prepares parents for home care
- Reduces readmission risk
- Supports healthy recovery
- Enhances parent-infant bonding
- Improves long-term outcomes

### Warnings and Important Notes

- **Review before discharge:** Ensure understanding
- **Ask questions:** Until instructions are clear
- **Written instructions:** Keep for reference
- **Follow-up appointments:** Attend all scheduled visits
- **Emergency signs:** Know when to seek immediate care
- **Resources:** Hospital discharge planning team available
